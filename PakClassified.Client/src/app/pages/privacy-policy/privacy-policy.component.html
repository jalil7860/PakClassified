Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect and manage your data.

Last Updated: October 19, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to PakClassified ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our vehicle marketplace platform.

By accessing or using PakClassified, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms, please do not access the platform.

Information We Collect

Personal Information
  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, profile picture
  • Contact Details: Phone numbers, WhatsApp contacts for communication
  • Location Data: City, area information for localized vehicle listings
Vehicle Listing Information
  • Vehicle details (make, model, year, price, features)
  • Vehicle images and descriptions
  • Contact information displayed in public listings
Automatically Collected Information
  • IP address, browser type, device information
  • Usage data and browsing behavior on our platform
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

How We Use Your Information

Vehicle Listings

Create and display your vehicle advertisements to potential buyers

User Communication

Facilitate communication between buyers and sellers

Platform Security

Protect against fraud and unauthorized activities

Customer Support

Provide assistance and resolve platform issues

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your contact information is shared with interested buyers when you create a vehicle listing
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate our platform (hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
